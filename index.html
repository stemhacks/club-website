Welcome Class of 2020!!

What is STEMHacks?

STEMHacks is our schools home for programming and computers. Here students will be able to learn how to code, be able to participate in hackathons and CTF's, and gain the opportunity to organize their very own hackathon

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a gathering where programmers collaboratively code in an extreme manner over a short period of time. Hackathons are at least a few days - or over a weekend - and generally no longer than a week. While working on a particular project, the idea is for each developer to be have the ability and freedom to work on whatever he/she wants.

What is our Hackathon

Our self titled hackathon, STEMHacks is one of the very first high-school hackathons in Pennsylvania who’s mission it is to inspire the youth to program. In 24 hours 200+ students from the tri-state area will create a project from scratch that will be judged from some of the leaders in the tech industry for a chance to win amazing prizes. With unlimited food, drink, interesting demos and workshops, and of absolutely no cost.

When do we meet?

During C-days

Where do we meet?

Last year the STEMHacks club would meet in room A003 or A004 but it really is dependent on circumstances. The club is very flexible but this will be updated once a meeting location has been solidified

Why should I join?

Here at STEMHacks you have to opportunity to learn programming a skill that is becoming a near necessity in today's world. You get to experience all different types of technologies and participate in lots of fun events.
Also you gain the incredibly educational and useful opportunity of organizing a hackathon where you build many invaluable lifeskills. Hackathons are universally accepted by both companies and colleges acting as a great item on your resume.

What will we do as a club?

During C-day meetings we will be conducting mini interactive classes on programming. Topics will vary between meetings.
We will be planning trips to go to various hackathons near us as a group throughout the year.
Also if you are interested you will be able to help with the organization our hackathon.
